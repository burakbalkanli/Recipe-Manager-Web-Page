Introduction

Recipe manager is an application for learning and sharing new recipes. With recipe manager, users will never lose their recipes again. Recipe manager allows their users to synchronize their accounts with multiple devices. Now users can easily create, update, and delete their recipes. Recipe manager also supports text, sound, and video formats. Users can create their recipes and give directions in the text and video format. Users will be logged in the application with a default rank which is Amateur. As they create new recipes, get likes and comments to their recipes, their ranking will be updated to Home Cook and Pro Chef. This application also gives a chance to users to interact with each other. As an example, an Amateur Cook can follow a Pro Chef and have a chance to chat with them or they can just use their recipes. Recipes will be categorized in different forms. Such as, difficulty level, food category and cooking time so that users can easily find the recipes they need. Recipe manager is also great for users who can't decide what to eat. With using this application, users can choose what to cook from recipe managers weekly meal suggestions.
